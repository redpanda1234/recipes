Recipe: Pasta salad with broccoli and olives

- Summary
  - Serves 6 to 8
  - If you prefer a spicier salad, increase the red pepper
    flakes.

+ Ingredients
  + 3 pounds broccoli (2 medium bunches), florets cut into
    bite-size pieces (7 cups)
  + 1/2 teaspoon grated lemon zest pluss 1/4 cup juice ( 2
    lemons)
  + Salt
  + 1 garlic clove, minced
  + 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  + 1 pound bite-size pasta such as fusilli, farfalle, or
    orchiette
  + 1/2 cup kalamata olives or other brine-cured variety,
    pitted and chopped
  + 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
