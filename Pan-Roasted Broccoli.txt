Recipe: Pan-roasted broccoli with lemon browned butter

- Summary
  - Serves 4
  - Watch the butter carefully as it cooks, as it can
    go from brown to black in a matter of seconds

+ Ingredients
  + 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  + 1 small shallot, minced
  + 1/4 teaspoon salt
  + 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  + 3/2 teaspoons lemon juice
  + 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
  + 1 recipe Pan-Roasted Broccoli
