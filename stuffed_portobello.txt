Recipe: Stuffed portobello mushrooms with spinach and
       goat cheese

- Summary info
  - Serves 4 as main course or 8 as side dish
  - When shopping, choose dense mushrooms with a cupped shape.
  - Blue cheese can be substituted for the goat cheese.
  - Buy whole portobello mushrooms for this recipe, not
    just the caps!

+ Ingredients
  + 10 portobello mushrooms (4 to 5 inches in diameter)
    stems removed and reserved
  + .25 cup olive oil
  + salt and pepper
  + 12 ounces baby spinach (~12 cups)
  + 2 tabelspoons water
  + 2 slices hearty white sandwich bread, torn into
    quarters
  + 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  + 2 onions, chopped fine.
  + 4 garlic cloves, minced
  + 1/2 cup dry sherry
  + 4 ounces goat cheese, crumbled (1 cup)
  + 1 cup walnuts, toasted and chopped coarse
  + 1/4 cup heavy cream
  + 2 tablespoons chopped fresh thyme
  + 2 tablespoons lemon juice
